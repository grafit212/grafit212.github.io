About


Concept artist and illustrator based in Moscow, Russia.
Work on game projects, illustrations and architectural designs. I have academic art education and extensive experience in traditional art.
Especially interested in environment concept art.

N-hance Studio - character/creature concept artist

WanderBuild - interior designer

Unannounced horror game in russian setting - environment concept artist

Project Alien Summer - illustrator and UI designer

A.Y.std, Force of Nature 2 - promo illustrator, character artist, UI and icons designer
